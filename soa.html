This Statement of Applicability (SoA) is a mandatory document required by ISO/IEC 27001:2022 clause 6.1.3(b). It declares which of the 93 Annex A controls are applicable to NovaTech GmbH's ISMS scope, the justification for each decision, and the current implementation status.

NovaTech GmbH is a simulated Berlin-based technology startup of 30 employees that develops an AI-powered customer analytics platform. The company processes personal data of EU residents acting as a data processor (GDPR Art. 28) and hosts its entire infrastructure on AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt). Controls marked as "Not applicable" are excluded because NovaTech operates no on-premises physical infrastructure. All exclusions are justified below and do not create unacceptable risks given the AWS-hosted environment.
